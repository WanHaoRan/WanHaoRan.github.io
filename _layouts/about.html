Haoran Wan  

I am a first year Ph.D. student in Computer Science at Princeton University, advised by Professor Kyle Jamieson. My recent research focuses on wireless telemetry.

Before Princeton, I got my M.E. degree in Computer Science and Technology at Nanjing University and worked with Professor Wei Wang on acoustic sensing.

I received my BEng degree from the School of Information and Communication Engineering of University of Electronic Science and Technology of China in 2019.
