Haoran Wan  

I am a M.S. student in the Department of Computer Science and Technology at Nanjing University. I am working with Professor Wei Wang. My recent research focuses on building acoustic sensing and Internet-of-Things applications (localization, interaction, vital sign monitoring/health care, etc.) on mobile devices (smart phone, smart speaker, VR/AR, etc.).

I received my BEng degree from the School of Information and Communication Engineering of University of Electronic Science and Technology of China in 2019.
